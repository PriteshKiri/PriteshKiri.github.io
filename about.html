About

Hi! My name is Himani Mehta, a freelance artist and graphic designer based in Mumbai,India. I specialize in Mandala design, doodle design, surface pattern design, Zentangle art, logo and branding.

I am an interior architect turned artist. Initially, I have worked for companies like Asian paints as an interior and colour consultant. As I was taking a break from corporate jobs, I stumbled onto mandala art, and since then I am hooked to making mandala.

I learnt a lot from this art form and now I am teaching others the importance of this art. As, Mandala is beneficial for mental health, I have conducted numerous sessions and workshops to bring light on the topic of mental health wellness. I have also collaborated with 5 mandala colouring books and this has really encouraged me to continue with this beautiful journey of mine. I have also worked with start-ups internationally for clothing and brand designing and have made logos for small business owners as well. Working internationally as well as locally, helped me gain a perspective of this art world and has also made me understand the impact different cultures possess on any form of art.
